Ringtone Maker & MP3 Cutter is the easiest way to turn any song or audio file on your phone into a custom ringtone, notification tone, or alarm.

✂️ PRECISE AUDIO TRIMMING
Pick your audio file, drag the start and end handles on the waveform to select exactly the section you want, then tap Trim. It's that simple.

🎵 LIVE WAVEFORM PREVIEW
See a full waveform visualization of your audio so you know exactly what you're cutting. Drag the handles with precision — no guesswork.

▶ PLAY BEFORE YOU CUT
Preview the trimmed section before saving to make sure it sounds perfect.

🔔 SET AS RINGTONE
Save your trimmed audio and set it directly as your phone ringtone, notification sound, or alarm tone — all from within the app.

📁 WORKS WITH ALL AUDIO FORMATS
Supports MP3, AAC, M4A, OGG, WAV, and more. Any audio file on your device works.

🌙☀ DARK & LIGHT MODE
A deep purple dark mode and a crisp light mode. Your preference is saved automatically.

PERFECT FOR:
• Making custom ringtones from your music library
• Cutting a specific lyric or drop as a notification sound
• Trimming audio clips for any purpose

No ads. Minimal permissions — only reads audio files you explicitly choose.
